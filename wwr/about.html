Clean Water Rafting Co. offers thrilling river adventures that inspire trust, teamwork, and fun. We connect people with nature through unforgettable experiences. Our motto: “Ride the Rapids, Respect the River.”

History

Clean Water Rafting Co. is your gateway to unforgettable river adventures, thrilling rapids, and nature-filled experiences. We specialize in guided rafting tours that combine safety, fun, and breathtaking scenery. Our mission is to inspire a love for the outdoors, promote teamwork, and build lasting memories through every splash and paddle.

Founded over a decade ago by a group of outdoor enthusiasts, Clean Water Rafting Co. began as a small passion project on local rivers. What started with a single raft and a love for whitewater quickly grew into a trusted adventure company known for its commitment to safety, sustainability, and unforgettable customer experiences. Over the years, we've guided thousands of thrill-seekers through scenic river landscapes, fostering a deep appreciation for nature and the joy of adventure.
